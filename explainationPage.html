An Explanation

In order to win a minesweeper game you must "find" the mines in the table.
At each stage, there are different numbers of mines that will be displayed
on the game page, to know where the mines are: first, click on one of the cells
in the game table. When you find a mine location, right-click the cell and you will immediately
see a warning sign in the cell to remind the player the places of the mines that he thinks.
Once you've checked all the mined cells you'll know if you were right ...
Be careful not to press a mine cell in case of a cell where a mine is forced to
start from the beginning.
Only after you have succeeded in the current level you can move on to the next level.
Hope you enjoy it, good luck!
